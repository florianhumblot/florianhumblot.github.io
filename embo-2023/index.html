emBO++ 2023 talk

Published: 2023-04-27

On the 25th of March 2023, I gave my first ever talk at a conference! This happened in Bochum, Germany, at emBO++ 2023. This conference is focused on embedded systems development and C++, I highly recommend watching the sessions of past years and of course, I also recommend going to the next emBO++ conference in person!

There I gave a talk on containerization of developer environments to “get rid of ‘it works on my machine’”. Luckily the conference organizers recorded all the sessions and posted them on Youtube for everyone to enjoy! You can find the full emBO++ 2023 playlist here.
